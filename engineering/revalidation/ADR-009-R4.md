# ADR-009 R4 Engineering-Rule Revalidation

Date: 2026-07-10

Source change:

- `OPEN_QUESTIONS.md V0.2.18` to `V0.2.20`;
- acceptance of `ADR-009 R4` and resolution of `SLICE-005`.

This record classifies canonical engineering rules under the source-bound rule
lifecycle. It records engineering consequences only and does not redefine the
accepted completion contract.

## Material Rule Changes

| Rule | Result | Reason |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `ENG-CLAIM-001 R2` | `R3` | Forbidden shapes and required checks now distinguish partial claim-class results, `P`, `D`, `A`, historical acceptance, and current claim standing under accepted `ADR-009 R4`. |
| `ENG-CLAIM-002 R2` | `R3` | Reservation checks now govern completion artifacts, prohibit retroactive formal-evidence promotion and self-certification, and preserve the separate `EVAL-004`/`EVAL-005` prerequisites. |
| `ENG-CHANGE-001 R2` | `R3` | The required trigger check removes resolved `SLICE-005` from unresolved-frontier control and adds compliance with its accepted resolution contract. |
| `ENG-CONF-CLAIM-001 R2` | `R3` | Selected-slice claim checks replace the stale `SLICE-005` trigger with the accepted exact-basis completion contract and its `EVAL-004`/`EVAL-005`, `P -> D -> A`, non-waiver, and claim-standing consequences. |

Each revision increment is required because the rule lifecycle treats a change
to a forbidden shape or required check as material. The targets remain the same,
so no new rule ID is required.
